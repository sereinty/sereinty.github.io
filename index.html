What is Cookie Run Kingdom?

Cookie Run: Kingdom (CRK) is an action role-playing gacha game based in South Korea by Devsisters. CRK was released publicly on January 19, 2021. In this game you play as a numerous cookies! Currently there are 167 playable cookies and more to come. You start off as a cookie named Gingerbrave who escaped a witch (which is how all cookies are baked) and join other cookies from all over the world to stop the plans of Dark Enchantress Cookie (one of the key villains of the story).

What type of features does CRK have?


  • Story Mode is the standard mode, in which the player follows the group of GingerBrave, Strawberry Cookie, Wizard Cookie, Custard Cookie III and Chili Pepper Cookie on the game's storyline. By completing story mode you learn the lore and get stars and awards such as: diamonds, coins (both currency within the game) and star jelly which levels up your cookies to be stronger. Currently there is 18 Story Mode chapters with each chapter having around 30 episodes!

  • Dark Mode is a more difficult optional mode with more awards! In order to obtain Dark Mode you must have gotten all stars in the Story Mode episode. Alongside the difficultly increase, players can earn Cookie Soulstones by clearing levels. While it uses the same overworld as Story Mode, it has a darker theme and levels have been renamed.

  • Master Mode is the hardest mode currently in Cookie Run: Kingdom's Story Mode. To get to Master mode, the player will have to finish Story Mode Episode 10. Players can earn Common, Rare, Epic, and Legendary 'Essences'/'Soulstones' when clearing the mode, which again makes your cookie stronger.

  • Arena Battle Arena Battle is an online mode where players can battle each other's cookie teams. Winning against other teams gives the player trophies and rank up points, which depending on how strong the team was- will vary. At the end of the season, the player's rank is reset and given a gift, which also varies, depending on the player's rank. Losing against other teams will make the player lose trophies. Players can have an attacking team and a defending team. Many people love to play this as at the end of each season you can obtain many diamonds depending on your rank.

  • Bounties are a game mode and is considered a material farming stage system. Players are able to select a five Cookie team and defeat varying levels of Skill Powders to level the Skills of their Cookies. Skill Powders come in three tiers of rarity.

  • Tower of Sweet Chaos Tower of Sweet Chaos is a permanent game mode in CookieRun: Kingdom, where players enter multiple levels of challenges to earn rewards. Some of these awards contain legendary cookies if you beat enough levels. It was released on 12 May 2021 in the Tiers of Chaos update, and is unlocked once a player clears Stage 4-12 in World Exploration.

  • Cookie Alliance is a game mode that is unlocked after clearing stage 10-31 in World Exploration. players create 5 teams of 5 cookies each to battle waves of enemies, with every 4 waves being a boss. There are 6 levels of increasing difficulty. The better a player does, the better rewards they get, such as Guild Relics and Artifact Points to make the team stronger, and other miscellaneous rewards. At the end of the season, players get rewards based on how many points the player and their guild earned that season.

  • Trial Grounds is a game mode where the player can obtain Life Jewels to create Crystal Jams (Magic Candy for Legendary Cookies). The player creates a team, which requires the cookie you are getting the Life Jewels for. Players get more Life Jewels for completing harder stages, and You get 7 battles a week, and you can get an extra 3 a week by buying the Kingdom Pass. Many things in this game can be pay to win.

  • Guild Battle is a game mode where you battle 3 bosses. Guilds work together to defeat the bosses, and when the guild defeats a boss, everyone gets rewards and the Guild gets Guild Trophies. At the end of the season, everyone in the Guild gets rewards based on the amount of Guild Trophies the Guild obtained.
  • Special Episodes help players understand the lore of the game more.

Characters

One of the best parts of the game in my opinion are the characters! They add heart to the game and without it, it would be very boring!

Thankfully as mentioned previously, Cookie Run Kingdom has 167 cookies currently.. WHICH IS A LOT! Each cookie is unique with a story, design and strength! This shows that DEVSISTERS is devoted to creating a wonderful experience for each cookie making the game more fun for players. CRK also centers a cookie's design, personality and overall character based on the flavour of the cookie. There is a good chance your favourite cookie flavour is a character!

Some of my favourite cookies are...

Black Licorice Cookie

Black Licorice Cookie is one of the minions of the evil Dark Enchantress Cookie (one of the major villains in the lore) and he is a magic type and considered a Epic in terms of rarity.

Like many players, I love this cookie! His apperance is very cool and he is considered a villain, naking his backstory just as cool as his apperance. He has a cool black robe with a licorice sythe. He is a playable character and summons cute licorice warriors to fight for the player's team which gives defense for the player's team.

The next cookie is Sea Fairy Cookie!

Sea Fairy Cookie is a bomber type cookie and has is considered a water element type. She reigns over the seas and controls all the oceans. She is considered a Legendary in terms of rarity and was the first legendary cookie added into the game.

I love this cookie as I started playing around 4 years ago when it laumched. Sea Fairy was one of the rarest cookies at the time which created a desire for her. She also has a very beautiful design and used to be META which means she used to extremelty powerful. Although Sea Fairy isn't META anymore, I still have love for her since she brings back nostalgic memories.

Finally, we have... Pure Vanilla Cookie!

Pure Vanilla is a healer type cookie and is considered an Ancient type in terms of rarity which is the strongest and rarest type in terms of rarity. Pure Vanilla is also is the founder and former king of the Vanilla Kingdom which is one of the strongest kingdoms in the lore.

I ADORE this cookie as his lore makes me understand as a player how sweet and caring he is! He is also a exceptional healer and considered META making him not only a likable character personality wise... but also in gamemodes!
